# Introduction

The objects we look at are vector spaces and linear operators.

Need:
1. Some basic linear algebra in general vector spaces.
2. Some basic `set theory`. In particular, many theorems depend on the `axiom of choice`.

We:
- briefly discuss that most controversial axiom of set theory and some equivalent statements
- look at the algebraic structure on a vector space and topologies - compatible with the algebraic structure - on them

## Content

1. Preliminary Material

2. Linear spaces, metric spaces, normed spaces.
